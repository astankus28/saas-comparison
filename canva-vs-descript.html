Pros · Canva

Extremely easy for non-designers; massive template library; robust free tier.

Cons

AI video output is low-res; lacks advanced 'pro' design features found in Adobe.

Pros · Descript

Edit video by deleting text (game changer); amazing noise removal; built-in recorder.

Cons

Can be buggy on longer video files; clunky for traditional frame-by-frame editing.

Who should pick which?

Choose Canva if your primary job is Visual Content & Social Media. Choose Descript if you are optimizing for Podcasters & Video Creators.
